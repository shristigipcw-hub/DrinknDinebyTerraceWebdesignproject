Our Story

Rooftop Elegance in the heart of Noida

Drink and Dine by Terrace isn’t just a restaurant — it’s a sensory escape on the 4th floor of Spectrum Metro Mall. Designed for dreamers, lovers, and connoisseurs, our space blends lush greenery, warm golden lights, and breathtaking open-air seating.

Whether you crave an intimate date under fairy lights or a vibrant group celebration, our curated multi-cuisine menu and handcrafted mocktails elevate every moment. Experience Noida’s most aesthetic rooftop destination.
